Exposure to biological agents
In some activities, such as carding and willowing, workers may be exposed to biological agents such as anthrax,
clostridium tetani (the causative agent for tetanus), and coxiella burnetti (which causes Q fever). Exposure to
biological agents can result in allergies and respiratory disorders.
